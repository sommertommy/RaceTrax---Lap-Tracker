Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

RC Race Tracker ("the app") respects your privacy.

## Information Collection
The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Camera Usage
The app uses the device camera to detect colors and track RC cars during races. 
All processing happens locally on the device. No camera data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services
The app may use third-party services such as Firebase to support functionality. 
These services do not receive any personal user data.

## Data Storage
All data created in the app (such as race setups) is stored locally on the device.
